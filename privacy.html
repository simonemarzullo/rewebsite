Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Simone Marzullo, a REALTOR® affiliated with The Agency in Beverly Hills, California ("we," "us," or "our"), collects, uses, and protects the personal information of visitors to this website (the "Site").

Information We Collect

When you use the forms on this Site — including the Home Valuation, Seller, Buyer, Investor, and Contact forms — we may collect information you voluntarily provide, such as:

  • Your name, email address, and phone number
  • Property addresses and property details
  • Any additional notes, questions, or messages you submit

We do not knowingly collect information from you unless you choose to submit it through one of our forms.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your inquiry and follow up regarding buying, selling, or valuing a property

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Information you submit may be stored and managed using a customer relationship management (CRM) platform, which we use to organize inquiries and follow up with you efficiently. We may also share information with The Agency and its affiliated personnel as needed to provide real estate services, and with service providers who help us operate this Site (such as web hosting). You may reply STOP to any text message to opt out of future texts, or contact us directly using the information below to stop receiving communications at any time.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to your inquiry, provide requested services, and comply with applicable legal, accounting, or reporting obligations.
