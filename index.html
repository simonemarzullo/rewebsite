About Simone

Built on Strategy.
Driven by Results.

With a global perspective shaped by years of international experience and over a decade of living in Los Angeles, Simone Marzullo brings a unique and competitive edge to The Agency Beverly Hills. Backed by deep market insight and the powerful resources of one of the world's top real estate firms, he delivers seamless, results-driven experiences for buyers, sellers, and investors alike.

From high-net-worth individuals and seasoned developers to first-time homebuyers, Simone tailors every strategy to meet the unique needs of each client. Known for building lasting relationships, he ensures every client feels supported, informed, and confident throughout the process.

With a thoughtful, hands-on approach and a relentless drive to achieve exceptional results, Simone Marzullo makes every real estate experience both strategic and rewarding.
